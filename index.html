Set the radio to 144.800 MHz, APRS ON, and configure your callsign first. iOS is not supported (its browsers lack Web Serial/WebUSB). On Android use Chrome + a USB-OTG cable. The radio must be in normal mode (not flash mode). Note: with a plain 3-wire programming cable, TX may be blocked unless the firmware's cable-voltage exception is present.
